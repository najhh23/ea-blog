Company Overview

Electronic Arts (EA) is a global leader in digital interactive entertainment. We develop and publish innovative games, services, and experiences that inspire players around the world to play, compete, and connect.

With iconic franchises such as EA SPORTS™, Battlefield™, The Sims™, Apex Legends™, and Need for Speed™, EA continues to push the boundaries of creativity and technology.

Executive Team

EA is led by a diverse group of experienced executives committed to innovation, inclusion, and long-term growth. Our leadership team shapes the vision that guides every game, studio, and community.

Under strong leadership, EA continues to evolve as a player-first company focused on creativity and responsible business practices.

EA Studios

EA Studios is a network of world-class game development studios located across North America, Europe, and Asia. Each studio brings its own culture, creativity, and expertise.

From cinematic storytelling to competitive multiplayer, our studios create unforgettable experiences for millions of players.

Careers at EA

At EA, we believe creativity thrives in diverse and inclusive environments. We empower our people to innovate, collaborate, and grow professionally.

Whether you're a developer, designer, storyteller, or technologist, EA offers opportunities to shape the future of entertainment.

Latest News

Stay updated with EA’s latest announcements, game launches, studio updates, and community initiatives. We continue to evolve our portfolio and expand our global reach.

Our news reflects our commitment to innovation, player engagement, and industry leadership.

Partners & Collaborators

EA Partners is the division within Electronic Arts that seeks out the best and boldest talent from independent studios around the world and partners with them to help deliver their incredible games to players across all platforms and devices.

For games under the EA Originals label, EA Partners supports, empowers and enables independent studios to create the games they are passionate about and we believe will delight our players. Our goal is to develop and foster relationships with studios that allow us to work in sync with their vision as we continue to innovate and strategically lead the industry into future ways to play.

We also build partnerships with independent studios to work with us on EA IP to deliver amazing new experiences to our players under the EA brand.
