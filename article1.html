THE RISE OF THE AZZURRI


As Spain made their debut as tournament hosts on the biggest international stage, Season 3 celebrates Italy’s rise from drawing their opening three matches to storming on to lift the trophy for a historic third time. Honour both nations with new Live Events, Objectives, and an all-new Season Ladder and Premium Pass for Season 3.


MORE SILVER SUPERSTARS


World Tour Silver Superstars continues to bring the themed action, celebrating the stars of Spain ‘82 with more historic Player Item rewards for Season 3. Take your best silver Player Items on the road and compete against other silver squads.


SEASON 3 EVO TOURNAMENT CUP


Showcase your favourite Evolutions as the Tournament Cup continues with a twist in Season 3. Introducing squad requirements for the inclusion of Evolution Items, build a squad of evolved Player Items and compete for rewards including Evolution Consumables, Cosmetics, and Packs.


NEW SEASON LADDER AND PREMIUM PASS


A new Season brings fresh rewards to the Season Ladder, featuring new World Tour Player Items from both the host and winning nations, Spain and Italy, as well as other themed rewards including a campaign ICON for the very first time in EA SPORTS FC™ 26.

Climbing the Standard Season Ladder will land you with a Ricardo Kaka Manager Career ICON, José Luís Gayà World Tour Player Item, World Tour Rarity Cosmetic EVO Consumable, Jockey + EVO Consumable, Nicolo Barella World Tour Player Item, and more.

Get the Premium pass for 500k Coins or 1000 FC Points and progress through the Premium Ladder to unlock 2,500 SP Consumable x2, 78+ x10 (Tradeable), Amaiur Sarriegi Thunderstruck Player Item, Low Driven Shot+ EVO Consumable, and Gianfranco Zola Winter Wildcards ICON, and more.
